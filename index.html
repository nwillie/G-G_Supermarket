About Us

We are a unique brand located in the Central Region, Kasoa.

  • HISTORY

    G-G Supermarket was started as a small table-top chocolate business by Mrs. Agyei in January 28th, 2019.

  • GOALS

    Promoting Ghana-made products has been the aim of Mrs. Agyei since the start of G-G. As a grande retailer of the chococoa and Ekumfi juice, G-G proves to have Ghana's economic growth at heart.

  • GROWTH

    As the biggest Supermarket in the country today and amongst the top in Africa, G-G Supermarket boasts of an exponential business growth and a clear accomplishment of set goals.

FAQs

Welcome to our supermarket! We’re committed to providing you with a seamless shopping experience. Whether you’re exploring our wide range of products or looking for the best deals, we’re here to assist you.

To help you navigate our services, we've compiled a list of frequently asked questions.If you need further assistance, don’t hesitate to reach out to our friendly staff. Happy shopping!

1.What are your store hours?

Our supermarket is open from 8 AM to 10 PM, seven days a week. Holiday hours may vary, so please check our website for updates.

2.Do you offer online shopping and delivery?

Yes! We offer online shopping with delivery options available. You can place your order through our website and choose a convenient delivery time.

3.What is your return policy?

We accept returns on most items within 24 hours of purchase with a receipt. Perishable items and certain hygiene products may be excluded.

4.Are there any loyalty programs or discounts available?

Yes, we have a loyalty program that rewards you with points for every purchase. You can also find weekly promotions and discounts on select items in-store and online.

5.How can I contact customer service?

You can reach our customer service team by calling the store directly, using the contact form on our website, or visiting the customer service desk during store hours. We’re here to help!
